# Poster Draft: "Campus Cup Challenge"

**Headline:** Help Reduce Waste! Bring Your Own Cup.

**Subheading:** Together we can make a difference.

**Body Text:**
Every day, thousands of disposable cups end up in landfill. You can help change this! By bringing a reusable cup to campus coffee shops, you are taking a stand for sustainability.

**Call to Action:**
- Bring your own cup every time you buy coffee.
- Encourage your friends to do the same.
- Be part of the solution, not the pollution.

**Visual Elements:**
- Image of students with reusable cups smiling.
- Icon of a reusable cup with a checkmark.
- University logo and sustainability office logo.

**Footer:** 
This campaign is brought to you by the Campus Sustainability Office. Let's work together for a greener campus!
